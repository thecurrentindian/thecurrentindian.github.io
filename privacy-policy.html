Privacy Policy for InGully

Last updated: August 17, 2023

Introduction

Welcome to InGully. We understand that privacy online is important to users of our website, especially when conducting business. This statement governs our privacy policies concerning those users of the website ("Visitors") who visit without transacting business and Visitors who register to transact business on the website and make use of the various services offered by InGully.

Personal Information About Visitors Collected by InGully

InGully does not collect any personal information about its Visitors.

How does InGully use personal information?

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not use it in any way.

Use of Cookies and Web Beacons

We use cookies to store information, such as your personal preferences when you visit our website. This could include showing a welcome message, presenting a unique web page, or tracking your viewing preferences.

A web beacon is a transparent graphic image used on websites and in emails that check user behavior visiting websites or sending emails. They are also referred to as web bugs and are commonly used by sites that use third-party services to monitor the traffic. They may be used in conjunction with cookies to understand how Visitors interact with the pages and content on the pages of a website.

We may also use cookies, beacons, or similar technologies for the purpose of tracking and understanding our Visitor journey, number of page visits, and the kind of interactions our Visitors have on our website.

Google Analytics

InGully uses Google Analytics 4 to understand user behavior and improve the user experience on the website. Google Analytics is a tool that captures data related to the demographics and interests of our Visitors. The tool uses a set of cookies to collect information and report website usage statistics without personally identifying individual Visitors.

If you wish to opt-out of Google Analytics tracking, please visit the Google Analytics opt-out page.

Privacy Policy Changes

InGully reserves the right to change its privacy policy as necessity dictates. Visitors are encouraged to frequently check this page for any changes to stay informed about how we are helping to protect the personal information we collect.

Acceptance of Terms

By using this website, you signify your acceptance of this policy. If you do not agree to this policy, please do not use our website. Your continued use of the website following the posting of changes to this policy will be deemed as your acceptance of those changes.

Contacting Us
